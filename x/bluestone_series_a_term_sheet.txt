SERIES A PREFERRED STOCK TERM SHEET

This Term Sheet summarizes the principal terms of the Series A Preferred Stock financing of AutoDrive AI, Inc., a Delaware corporation (the "Company").

DATE: March 10, 2024

INVESTORS:
Lead Investor: BlueStone Ventures ("Lead Investor")
Co-Investors: TechHorizon Fund, Angel Syndicate

AMOUNT OF FINANCING:
An aggregate of $5,000,000 (the "Investment Amount"), representing a pre-money valuation of $20,000,000.

TYPE OF SECURITY:
Series A Preferred Stock (the "Series A Preferred").

PRICE PER SHARE:
$2.50 per share (the "Original Issue Price"), based on a fully diluted capitalization of 10,000,000 shares prior to the financing.

LIQUIDATION PREFERENCE:
In the event of any liquidation, dissolution or winding up of the Company, the holders of Series A Preferred shall be entitled to receive in preference to the holders of Common Stock an amount per share equal to one times (1x) the Original Issue Price plus declared and unpaid dividends (the "Liquidation Preference"). After the payment of the Liquidation Preference, the remaining assets shall be distributed ratably to the holders of Common Stock.

CONVERSION:
The Series A Preferred shall be convertible, at the option of the holder, at any time, into shares of Common Stock at an initial conversion ratio of 1:1. The Series A Preferred shall automatically convert into Common Stock upon (i) the closing of a Firm Commitment Underwritten Public Offering with gross proceeds of at least $30,000,000, or (ii) the written consent of the holders of a majority of the then-outstanding shares of Series A Preferred.

DIVIDENDS:
Dividends will be paid on the Series A Preferred on an as-converted basis when, as, and if paid on the Common Stock. Non-cumulative.

VOTING RIGHTS:
The Series A Preferred shall vote together with the Common Stock on an as-converted basis, and not as a separate class, except (i) the Series A Preferred as a class shall be entitled to elect one (1) member of the Board of Directors (the "Series A Director"), and (ii) as required by law.

BOARD OF DIRECTORS:
The Board of Directors shall consist of five (5) members:
1. The CEO of the Company (Founder).
2. One representative elected by the holders of Common Stock.
3. One representative elected by the Lead Investor (Series A Director).
4. Two independent directors mutually agreed upon by the Common and Series A holders.

PROTECTIVE PROVISIONS:
So long as at least 25% of the Series A Preferred remains outstanding, the Company will not, without the written consent of the holders of at least a majority of the Company’s Series A Preferred, either directly or by amendment, merger, consolidation, or otherwise:
(i) Liquidate, dissolve or wind–up the affairs of the Company.
(ii) Amend, alter, or repeal any provision of the Certificate of Incorporation or Bylaws in a manner adverse to the Series A Preferred.
(iii) Create or authorize the creation of any new class or series of shares having rights, preferences or privileges senior to or on parity with the Series A Preferred.
(iv) Purchase or redeem any Common Stock (other than stock repurchased from former employees or consultants in connection with the cessation of their employment/services).
(v) Increase or decrease the authorized number of directors constituting the Board of Directors.

INFORMATION RIGHTS:
The Company will deliver to each Major Investor (Investors who invest at least $500,000):
(i) Annual, unaudited financial statements within 90 days after the end of each fiscal year.
(ii) Quarterly, unaudited financial statements within 45 days after the end of each quarter.
(iii) An annual budget and business plan at least 30 days prior to the beginning of each fiscal year.

RIGHT OF FIRST REFUSAL:
Major Investors shall have the right to participate on a pro rata basis in subsequent issuances of equity securities by the Company.

EXCLUSIVITY:
The Company agrees not to solicit or initiate any negotiations for any financing or sale of the Company for a period of 45 days from the date this Term Sheet is signed.

CONFIDENTIALITY:
This Term Sheet and its terms are confidential and shall not be disclosed by the Company or the Investors to any third party (other than their respective attorneys, accountants, and advisors) without the prior written consent of the other parties.

NON-BINDING:
This Term Sheet is not a commitment to invest, and is conditioned on the completion of due diligence, legal review, and execution of definitive agreements. This Term Sheet shall be governed by the laws of the State of Delaware.
