EXECUTIVE EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Executive Employment Agreement (the "Agreement") is made effective as of February 1, 2024 (the "Effective Date"), by and between OmniTech Innovations, Inc. (the "Company") and Michael Ross (the "Executive").

1. POSITION AND DUTIES.
   1.1 Position. The Company agrees to employ the Executive as its Chief Marketing Officer ("CMO"). The Executive shall report to the Chief Executive Officer ("CEO") and shall perform such duties and responsibilities as are customarily performed by persons in such position and as may be assigned by the Board of Directors.
   1.2 Full-Time Efforts. Executive agrees to devote his full business time, attention, and best efforts to the performance of his duties and to the furtherance of the Company's interests. Executive shall not engage in any other business activity, whether or not for gain, profit or other pecuniary advantage, without the prior written consent of the CEO.

2. COMPENSATION AND BENEFITS.
   2.1 Base Salary. The Company shall pay the Executive an initial annual base salary of $250,000 (the "Base Salary"), payable in accordance with the Company's standard payroll schedule. The Base Salary will be subject to annual review by the Board of Directors.
   2.2 Annual Bonus. Executive will be eligible to receive an annual performance bonus with a target amount equal to 20% of the Base Salary (the "Target Bonus"), based upon the achievement of objective or subjective criteria established by the Board.
   2.3 Equity Grant. Subject to approval by the Board, the Executive shall be granted an option to purchase 50,000 shares of the Company's Common Stock. The option shall vest over a four-year period, with 25% vesting on the first anniversary of the Effective Date and 1/48th of the total shares vesting monthly thereafter, subject to Executive’s continued employment.
   2.4 Benefits. Executive shall be eligible to participate in the employee benefit plans and executive compensation programs maintained by the Company, including (without limitation) health, life, dental, and vision insurance, 401(k) plan, and paid time off, in accordance with the terms of such plans.

3. TERMINATION OF EMPLOYMENT.
   3.1 At-Will Employment. Executive’s employment with the Company is "at-will." Both the Company and Executive may terminate the employment relationship at any time, with or without cause and with or without notice.
   3.2 Termination Without Cause or Resignation for Good Reason. If the Company terminates Executive’s employment without Cause (as defined below) or if Executive resigns for Good Reason (as defined below), and provided Executive executes a general release of claims in favor of the Company:
       (a) The Company shall pay Executive severance equal to six (6) months of his then-current Base Salary.
       (b) The Company shall pay a pro-rated portion of the Annual Bonus for the year in which termination occurs.
       (c) All unvested equity awards that would have vested during the six (6) month period following termination shall immediately accelerate and vest.
   3.3 Definition of "Cause". For purposes of this Agreement, "Cause" means: (i) Executive’s unauthorized use or disclosure of Company confidential information; (ii) Executive’s material breach of any agreement with the Company; (iii) Executive’s conviction of, or plea of "guilty" or "no contest" to, a felony or a crime involving moral turpitude; or (iv) Executive’s gross negligence or willful misconduct.
   3.4 Definition of "Good Reason". "Good Reason" means: (i) a material reduction in Executive’s Base Salary (unless pursuant to a salary reduction program applicable generally to the Company’s senior executives); (ii) a material diminution in Executive’s authority, duties, or responsibilities; or (iii) a relocation of Executive’s principal place of employment by more than 50 miles.

4. RESTRICTIVE COVENANTS.
   4.1 Non-Solicitation. During the Term and for a period of twelve (12) months following termination of employment, Executive shall not, directly or indirectly, solicit, induce, recruit or encourage any of the Company’s employees to leave their employment within the Company.
   4.2 Non-Competition. To the fullest extent permitted by applicable law, Executive agrees that during the term of employment, Executive will not engage in any employment, business, or activity that is in any way competitive with the business or proposed business of the Company.

5. MISCELLANEOUS.
   5.1 Arbitration. Any dispute, controversy or claim arising out of or relating to this Agreement, or the breach thereof, shall be settled by arbitration administered by the American Arbitration Association under its Employment Arbitration Rules.
   5.2 Entire Agreement. This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties resulting to the subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior or contemporaneous understandings, agreements, representations, and warranties, both written and oral.
